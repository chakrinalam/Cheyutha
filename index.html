About Us

Our founder's message


Cheyuta Swachhanda Seva Samsta is inspired by the patriotism of SWAMI VIVEKANANDA, the service motto of MOTHER TERESA, the love of our parents and the values given by our teachers to support the less fortunate people around us. We believe that "helping hands are better than praying lips" as Mother Teresa said! Cheyuta Swachhanda Seva Samsta is a ray of sunshine in today's scenario where we're slowly loosing the sight of basic human relations.


CHEYUTHA AS STUDENT'S FRIEND: Cheyuta also brings awareness over ethical values amongst young people. Encourages them in promoting social service and also letting them know that suicides are unethical.

CHEYUTHA CHIRUDEEPAM: It is very beautiful to make a child smile. So cheyuta takes privilege in celebrating auspicious Indian festivals such as Diwali, Christmas, Eid etc with little children from orphanages,etc who usually don't get to celebrate them so grandly.

Vision

Honest service, 'NO' to partiality based service, fastest possible service to the needy without any involvement of mediators(cheyuta team members take time to go collect the facts themselves about people in genuine need).

Mission

We're for equality in society, social well-being, to walk towards helping the less privileged as much as possible and strive to become a ray of hope to the needy among us.

Objective

Small employees, employees, teachers, social workers and many other service oriented hearts including students.

This initiative focuses on the profound joy of sharing meals with those in need, emphasizing the importance of personal involvement rather than merely providing financial contributions. Our mission is to engage directly with the community by preparing and distributing food to the hungry, fostering a sense of connection and compassion. We share our experiences and moments through photographs in the Cheyuta WhatsApp groups.
Additionally, we celebrate special occasions, such as birthdays and other significant events, at orphanages and senior care facilities, where we come together to share a meal with the residents, creating memorable experiences for all involved.
Cheyuta also responds to calls from weddings and other events to collect surplus food, ensuring that it is redistributed to those in need and local welfare organizations. Through these efforts, we aim to make a meaningful impact in our community, one meal at a time.

Cheyutha - Committed to Exceptional Service in the Fight Against Thalassemia.
Thalassemia is a serious genetic blood disorder caused by mutations in the DNA of cells responsible for producing hemoglobin — the protein in red blood cells that carries oxygen throughout the body. These mutations are inherited and passed from parents to their children.
Common symptoms of Thalassemia include:
Fatigue
Weakness
Pale or yellowish skin
Facial bone deformities
Slow growth in children
Abdominal swelling
Dark-colored urine

Cheyutha - Leading the Way in Organ Donation Awareness.
Cheyutha actively takes the lead in spreading awareness about organ donation across both rural and urban communities. In the 21st century, it's time to rise above myths and misconceptions.
Organ donation is not just a gift — it's a legacy.
By choosing to donate, you can give someone a second chance at life and make a lasting difference in the world.
Let's come together to build a future where no life is lost waiting for an organ.
Be a donor. Be a hero.


Cheyutha - Extending Medical Support to Those Who Need It Most.
Cheyutha regularly organizes medical awareness camps to educate people and reduce their vulnerability to diseases. Prevention is the first step toward a healthier life.
Is life only for those who can afford it? Absolutely not.
Everyone — rich or poor — has the right to live with dignity and access proper medical care.
That's why Cheyutha identifies underprivileged families in need of medical support and helps them afford necessary treatments.
To ensure genuine support reaches the right people, a fact-finding committee thoroughly investigates each case — even consulting local community members — before assistance is provided.
Cheyutha stands for compassion, integrity, and the right to live..


Cheyutha - Empowering Farmers, Promoting Sustainable Agriculture.
Cheyutha proudly supports the backbone of our nation — our farmers, the very reason we enjoy every meal.
Through various initiatives and programs, Cheyutha actively promotes organic farming and sustainable agricultural practices to ensure a healthier future for both people and the planet.
In addition, Cheyutha plays a key role in raising awareness about government schemes designed to uplift and support farmers, ensuring they benefit from every opportunity available to them.
Cheyutha also helps farmers market and sell their produce at fair prices, directly within their local communities — eliminating exploitation and ensuring they receive the value they truly deserve.
Cheyutha stands with farmers — for a greener tomorrow and a fairer today.


Cheyutha - Building Stronger Villages, One Sunday at a Time
Cheyutha has initiated a weekly awareness program, conducted every Sunday in different villages, in collaboration with local elders and youth.
This initiative embraces an integrated approach to rural development, focusing not just on improving physical infrastructure, but also on nurturing a strong sense of community and cooperation.
The goal is to inspire active public participation through:
-Mutual cooperation
- Self-help and self-reliance
- Improved standard of living and quality of life
- Environmental consciousness
Cheyutha believes that sustainable development must begin at the grassroots — and that positive, replicable models can emerge when communities work together.
Where possible, Cheyutha leverages existing government schemes and resources to strengthen and accelerate progress in these villages.
Together, we can build empowered, self-reliant communities.


Cheyutha - Education is a Right, Not a Privilege
The first step toward improving education for underprivileged children is to ignite hope and enthusiasm among their parents.
No child should be denied education due to financial hardship.
Cheyutha is committed to this belief. Through a fact-finding committee that closely works within local communities, Cheyutha identifies talented children who are forced to discontinue their education solely because of financial constraints.
Once verified, Cheyutha steps in to support and sponsor these children, helping them continue their education and chase their dreams.
Because every child deserves a chance to learn, grow, and thrive — regardless of their background.
